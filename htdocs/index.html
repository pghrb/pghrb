Pittsburgh.rb is a Ruby language user group for beginners and experts. Join us the first Thursday of every month at IBM in Squirrel Hill.

When

We have a regular monthly meeting on the first Thursday of each month:

6:30
Ruby help & discussion
7:30
Regular meeting

Please see our our Meetup.com group for information about our next monthly meeting.

We also hold coding nights under the OpenHack banner at the end of each month. For information on these visit the OpenHackPGH website.

Where

We regularly meet at IBM in Squirrel Hill.

IBM
1710 Murray Ave
Pittsburgh, PA 15217

Go in the same door as the Silk Elephant and take the stairs or elevator to the 3rd floor.

Learn

Interested in learning Ruby? Join us at 6:30 pm the night of a meeting for Ruby Help & Discussion hour, or check out some of these great sites:

  • _why's (poignant) guide to ruby (pdf)
  • tryruby
  • Ruby in 20 Minutes
  • Jumpstart Lab Tutorials
  • Ruby Koans
  • Codecademy Ruby Track
  • Learn Ruby the Hard Way
  • Pragmatic Programmer's Guide to Programming Ruby
  • Ruby Monk
  • Rails Guides @ RubyonRails.org
  • Ruby Warrior, a web-based game that teaches Ruby

Have another great resource? Let us know!
